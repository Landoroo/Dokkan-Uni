About & Rules Section

About

We decided to make this website because we have noticed that the Dokkan community doesn't have a strategy guide for events or mock teams for those events. Sure you have Reddit and other various websites, but why wait hours to get a response when you can come here and get answers immediately! We hope you enjoy your time spent on the website, if you have any questions, concerns, comments, or have a need to state improvements we need to make, please refer to our feedback page.

This is a free browsing website, meaning we will not force you to sign up, however, if you want notifications from the website making an account is required. You may also give feedback without signing up.

This platform only supports English speaking users as we do not have translators, we are sorry for the inconvenience.

We will do everything in our power to keep this website as user friendly as possible. :)

Rules

Keep feedback clean and respectful, there is a difference between constructive criticism and hatefulness.

You cannot directly comment on others feedback, but instead of filling the feedback with the same criticisms you can like or dislike others' critics letting us know if the change is necessary.

You may only make one account for every device you own, that the website works on, so that one person doesn't make a surplus of accounts to dislike one comment.

In the feedback section you may also add a screenshot of a mock team that you think is better than one of the ones we have on the site. We will review the team, likes and dislikes will not apply to this attribute as anyone can and will be bias, including ourselves.

Do not troll us, we appreciate constructive criticisms and legit questions, but trolling wastes our time as well as other users' time.
